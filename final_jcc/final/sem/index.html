Wise Choice Process

  1. What is my present situation?

    2. I don’t want to do work today..

  2. How would I like my situation to be?

    3. To not have the career I currently have & get the intended Degree.

  3. Do I have a choice here?

    4. I don’t know, maybe.

  4. What are my possible choices?

    1. Do the work & get the degree.
    2. Do the work but not get the degree.
    3. Make excuses & get out of doing actual work.
    4. Call in sick for the day.

  5. What’s the likely outcome of each possible choice?

    1. I actually Locked-In my future.
    2. I sort of locked-In my future. Probably won’t have the intended career but did provide for my family.
    3. The customer doesn’t get the services they requested.
    4. Would have to face tomorrow which then would lead to me possibly not having a job.

  6. Which choice(s) will I commit to doing?

    7. The first one, possible d depending on my mood & how I feel.
